What to Include in Your Service Request

Better request details lead to faster route confirmation and a more accurate initial conversation. Include the following when submitting:

  • ZIP Code — the most important field for confirming the route.
  • Property Type — residential home, restaurant, office, warehouse, multi-unit, or other commercial space.
  • Pest Issue — what you are seeing, where, and how often. A photo description or file helps significantly.
  • Urgency Level — normal request, urgent callback needed, or time-sensitive event like a health inspection or outdoor event.
  • Preferred Contact Window — morning, afternoon, or a specific date range that works for access.

Elite serves properties across the Greater Philadelphia tri-state area but confirms each route individually — exact coverage is validated by ZIP code, not just county or city name. Properties just outside the listed zones are welcome to submit a request; Elite will review and respond.
